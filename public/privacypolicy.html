Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 07, 2025

Introduction

At DataHive.co.in, operated by DataHive, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you use our website, services, or make payments through our payment gateway, Razorpay. It also covers our data-selling platform, which involves the collection, processing, and sale of anonymized public data for commercial purposes. By using our services, you agree to the terms outlined in this policy.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: When you sign up for our lead generation services, purchase premium leads, engage with our customer nurturing tools, or access our data-selling platform, we collect your name, email address, phone number, and billing address.
  • Payment Information: For payment processing, we collect necessary details through Razorpay, such as transaction data. We do not store your credit card, debit card, or bank account details on our servers.
  • Lead and Customer Data: As part of our lead prospecting and customer funnel services, we may collect business-related data such as company details, industry preferences, and prospect interactions.
  • Public Data: For our data-selling platform, we collect publicly available data from sources such as business directories, government publications, open web sources, and licensed third-party providers. This includes:
    • Business information (e.g., company names, addresses, industry classifications).
    • Demographic statistics and market trends.
    • Social media insights and public domain data.
  • Usage Data: We collect information about your interactions with our site, including IP address, browser type, pages visited, and timestamps, to improve our services.

All public data is anonymized to exclude personally identifiable information unless explicit consent is obtained from the data subject.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

  • Service Delivery: To provide lead generation, customer nurturing strategies, premium data solutions, and access to our 100 cr database for trading and sales leads.
  • Data-Selling Platform: To aggregate, process, and sell anonymized public data for commercial purposes such as market research, competitive analysis, and targeted advertising. Public data is cleaned, validated, and enriched to ensure accuracy and relevance, with no personally identifiable information included unless legally authorized.
  • Payment Processing: We use Razorpay to securely process payments for all services, including data purchases. Your payment data is encrypted and handled per PCI-DSS standards.
  • Communication: To send updates, newsletters, or alerts about your leads, customer nurturing campaigns, data purchases, or transactions. You can opt out of non-essential communications by clicking the unsubscribe link in our emails.
  • Analytics: To analyze user behavior and improve our platform’s functionality, including optimizing our CRM lead-to-opportunity process and data-selling platform.

Payment Processing with Razorpay

We use Razorpay as our payment gateway to process all transactions securely, including payments for public data purchases. Razorpay adheres to PCI-DSS standards to protect your payment information. We do not store your card data on our servers; it is encrypted and managed by Razorpay. For more details, please refer to Razorpay’s Privacy Policy.

Data Sharing and Consent

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties except:

  • With Razorpay, to process payments (with your explicit consent).
  • With service providers who assist in lead generation, customer nurturing, or data processing, only with your consent and for the purpose of delivering our services.
  • As required by law or to comply with legal obligations.

For our data-selling platform, we sell aggregated and anonymized public data that does not contain personal information unless explicitly authorized by the data subject. Before sharing any personal data with merchants or partners (e.g., for lead nurturing campaigns), we obtain your explicit consent through secure OTP verification or opt-in mechanisms. All data-sharing practices comply with the Information Technology Act, 2000 (India) and other applicable regulations, including adherence to principles of data minimization and transparency.

Data Security

We implement industry-standard measures to protect your personal and public data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. These include encryption, secure servers, and regular security audits. For public data, we ensure that any personally identifiable information is anonymized or removed before sale, in compliance with data protection laws. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your personal information by contacting us at support@datahive.co.in.
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time.
  • Request erasure of your personal data, after which we will delete all stored information from our servers, except where required by law.
  • Request information about the public data we have collected and sold, including its sources and purposes.
